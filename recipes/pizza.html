How to make pizza

Ingredients

  • cheese
  • tomato paste
  • pizza bread
  • pepperoni slices

Steps

  1. tomato paste on pizza bread
  2. sprinkle cheese on top
  3. add pepperoni slices on top of the cheese
  4. place pizza in over at 450 degrees for 20 minutes
  5. let cool down and enjoy !
